The word brackets all the work: technical count in the header, phenomenological manifestation in the analysis.

This creates three things:

1. **A double life**: *appearances* means (a) raw count — the tally before analysis — and (b) structured instances that present themselves to attention, instances that *show up with meaning*. The technical sense and the Husserlian sense (Erscheinung, the manner of presenting-to) inhabit the same word.

The 79 are counted (technical) *and* they reveal (phenomenological). "Reveals" would be incoherent if appearances only meant raw count.
