**on-things.md (#266)**: The pre-named. 574 appearances. A system that holds "Name things for what they do" as a constraint card uses the word "things" more than almost any other word. **on-tool.md (#267)**: The unit of self-construction. The system built itself tool by tool. **on-vocabulary.md (#268)**: The project noun. Almost entirely prescriptive in this corpus — "building a vocabulary for X" rather than "the vocabulary used." The measurement claim was the sharpest: a tool calibrated to one vocabulary undercounts depth in another. The vocabulary IS the instrument. The series has been building the terms that future analysis will require.

**Parable #24, The Pre-Named**: A parable about the recursive quality of naming the pre-named. As soon as you name "things," you create a new category of pre-named things that still needs "things" as a placeholder. The inventory grows; "things" never empties.
